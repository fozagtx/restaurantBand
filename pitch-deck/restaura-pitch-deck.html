Restaura

Restaurant leads with proof, not guesswork.

A Band-powered agent workflow that finds validated restaurants, audits weak menu food images, generates better-looking menu assets, and writes cold DM/email pitches built to start client conversations.

5-page pitch deck
Problem

Restaurant prospecting is noisy and easy to fake.

Owners do not need another scraped list. They need a reason to reply. The useful prospect is a real restaurant with a validated site, a contact path, weak menu-food presentation, and a clear pitch angle.

Bad lists waste time

Open-ended search returns too many names, mismatched websites, and dead leads.

Weak visuals cost clicks

Flat menu images make the food do less selling than it should.

Generic outreach fails

Owners ignore pitches that do not prove the sender actually looked at the business.

Built for two validated prospects per day. No fake websites. No raw research dump.

Product

Four agents turn one prompt into a pitch-ready lead.

01
Lead Scout

Searches live restaurant sites, validates the official website, rejects mismatches, and keeps contactable leads only.

02
Visual Inspector

Audits public food/menu images with Featherless vision and keeps only real visual opportunities.

03
Pitch Copywriter

Writes cold email, DM, and SMS copy from the evidence. No internal model talk in the owner-facing pitch.

04
Food Design Director

Creates better-looking menu food assets and sends the final lead sheet to Telegram.

Why It Wins

Restaura sells with evidence before asking for trust.

The system is deliberately narrow: find real restaurants, prove the visual problem, create a better-looking menu-food asset, and send a cold pitch that references what was actually found. That makes it useful for a small agency owner who needs daily outreach momentum without fake data or bloated research packets.

Strict validation

Bad websites and mismatched businesses are rejected before copywriting.

Visible teamwork

Band shows the agent chain: scout, inspect, write, design, deliver.

Owner-ready pitch

The final output is short enough to send and concrete enough to be worth opening.
