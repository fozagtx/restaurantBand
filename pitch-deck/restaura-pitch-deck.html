Daily outreach workflow

Restaura

A Band agent workflow for daily restaurant outreach: find real restaurants, inspect menu-food images, create good-looking food assets, and write cold DM/email pitches.

5-page pitch deck
Job

Find two restaurants worth pitching today.

The target user is a small menu design or restaurant marketing owner. They need a short daily list of restaurants where the food photos or menu presentation create a clear opening for a paid visual upgrade.

Real business

The official website loads, matches the restaurant name, and has enough public evidence for outreach.

Visual opening

The food or menu images have a specific issue the pitch can reference.

Contact route

The lead has a phone, email, social link, form, or named person for outreach.

Daily target: two checked leads with a pitch angle, a generated food asset, and sendable copy.

Agent Chain

Each agent owns one stage.

01
Lead Scout

Parses the Band prompt, searches Exa, validates the restaurant site, and hands candidates to inspection.

02
Visual Inspector

Reviews public image URLs with Featherless vision and writes the image issue in plain language.

03
Pitch Copywriter

Writes subject lines, cold email, DM, and SMS using the restaurant evidence.

04
Food Design Director

Creates a menu-food image asset and sends the final packet to Telegram.

Use Case

A daily prospecting desk for menu design services.

Restaura is built for a service owner selling restaurant menu images, food photo upgrades, and social-ready food assets. The workflow keeps the lead count tight, checks the website, names the image issue, creates a sample direction, and writes the first message.

Small daily batch

The workflow focuses on a couple of checked restaurants per day.

Visible handoff chain

Band shows scout, inspect, write, design, and delivery stages.

Sales asset included

The generated food image gives the pitch something concrete to show.
